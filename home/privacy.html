VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C.

In compliance with the Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (the "Law"), VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., protects and safeguards your personal information to prevent damage, loss, destruction, theft, misplacement, alteration, as well as unauthorized processing of the same.

We hereby inform you of the terms and conditions of the Privacy Notice of Personal Data ("Privacy Notice") of VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. residing at Vía Lombardía 5705 Int 503, Saucito, Chihuahua, Estado de Chihuahua, México.

What information do we collect?

The data that you freely and willingly provide to VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., either online, in writing, by telephone and/or by other means, may include but will not be limited to: (i) personal data, (ii) employment data, and (iii) assets and financial data. Also and only if applicable, we may collect some other personal data and employment data as appropriate, from third parties, such as your children, spouse, as well as personal references for consultation and verification of your data by VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. To make sure that the personal information you freely provide is accurate and updated data, VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. may collect information from other sources such as credit reporting companies, etc.

Similarly, and in accordance with VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., we may require a copy of your photo ID and proof of address.

In the case of personal data of third parties which we may collect through you, we likewise take security and confidentiality measures in treating their information in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Privacy Notice.

You declare express consent of your personal data being treated according to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Notice.

What do we use your information for?

We use the personal data collected for purposes including but not limited to: (i) identification and verification, (ii) contacting you, (iii) providing and delivering goods and services, (iv) identifying purchase history, (v) acquiring from you goods or services, and other similar purposes. In collecting and processing personal data that you provide, we comply with all the principles established by the Law (Article 6): legality, quality, consent, information, purpose, fairness, proportionality and accountability.

Who we share your data with?

VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. may retain the services of one or more third parties as service providers selected to support promotion and marketing activities of our goods and services, for management and administration of personal data that is collected online, or by any other means, as well as for verification of the information you provide, therefore VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. may even transfer it to said third parties, without a commercial purpose, but only to comply with the provision of contracted services. Similarly, VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., in no case will trade, sell or rent personal information about you to third parties without your prior consent.

It is understood that you have granted consent to transfer your personal data, if you don't explicitly express objection for the same being transferred.

Which security and control measures we use to protect your personal data?

VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. has implemented administrative, technical and physical security measures to protect your personal data, the same which we similarly demand be complied with by the service providers we retain.

Protecting the privacy of the personal data is particularly important for VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., for this reason when we have knowledge of personal data, its treatment will be carried out complying at all times with the guidelines and requirements established by Law.

What is the area responsible for the management and administration of personal data?

The responsible for the management and administration of personal data is: "Managing Partner", who can be reached by e-mail to contact@vmlaw.mx or directly at our office in San Felipe 205B, San Felipe I, Chihuahua, Chihuahua, México, C.P. 31203.

How do you exercise your ARCO rights and revoke the consent granted?

In terms of the Law, you may initiate the ARCO rights protection procedure at the Federal Institute of Access to Information and Protection of Personal Data (IFAI) within 15 days following the date at which VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. responds to your request or if by the time the term stipulated by Law has elapsed, VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C. has not yet responded your request.

As holder of personal data, you may exercise the ARCO (access, rectification, cancellation and opposition to the processing of personal data) rights or revoke the consent you have granted to VILLALOBOS & MOORE, S.C., for the treatment of your personal data by directly sending your request to "Managing Partner" area through the e-mail account: contact@vmlaw.mx. Said request should contain at least: (a) name and address or other means to communicate the answer to your request; (b) documents proving your identity or, where appropriate, legal representation; (c) clear and precise description of personal data, in relation to which exercise of any of the ARCO rights is requested, (d) express statement to revoke your consent to the processing of personal data and therefore, to prevent use of the same; (d) any other circumstance that facilitates the location of personal data.
